FANUC Welding Simulation

OBJECTIVE:

  • Create an automated sorting and packing system using Allen Bradley PLC and Studio 5000.
  • Allow operators to control product flow from a feeder to one of two lines (A or C), based on product size.
  • Enable packaging into specified set sizes (5, 7, 9, or 11), depending on which line is selected.
  • Ensure system safety using E-stops and logic conditions.

PROCESS:

  • Feeding Products:
    • Operator fills the feeder with up to 20 products using a momentary push button that increments a counter.
    • A green button moves the product from the feeder to a conveyor belt with a diverter.
  • Sorting Products:
    • Two push buttons determine whether the product should be sent to line A or C.
    • Two selector switches choose the set size:
      • Line A: Sets of 5 or 7
      • Line C: Sets of 9 or 11
  • Completion and Reset:
    • Once all 4 sets are full, a red pilot light signals completion.
    • Operator must press a reset button to zero the counters and restart the process.
  • Safety Features:
    • Two E-stops located at different points of the long conveyor system ensure quick shutdown access.
    • Seal-in contacts used in ladder logic prevent motor restart after power loss.
    • Products cannot be added to the feeder while the conveyor motor is running.

RESULT:

  • Designed and implemented a fully functional, operator-safe sorting and packing system.
  • Strengthened PLC programming skills using Studio 5000 and ladder logic.
  • Integrated user input, safety logic, and efficient process control in a real-world system.
